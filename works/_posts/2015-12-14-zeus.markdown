Out of the several Startup weekends / Hackaton projects I did [Zeus][zeus] is probably the most interesting one. It was developed during a three day Startup Weekend in Berlin in October 2015. 

Zeus is basically portable-USB-power-banks as a service: local shops (such as bars, 24/7, groceries, tourist info points) act as pick-up and delivery points for USB power banks that can be rented out in one place and returned to another one. During the three days project we developed a business model to market the service and run market researches to validate the product. 

![Zeus logo](/images/zeus/zeus_logo.png)
*the logo*

The brand name and logo play around Zeus - the Greek god - and to the characteristic lighting strikes that he shoots as arrows: the business that we developed was indeed a way to carry electricity around to charge a mobile device.
