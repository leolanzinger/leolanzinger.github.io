LinkU is a mobile application developed in 2015 in Paris for the Business Development Lab course at Université Paris-Sud, where I studied the first year of my Master degree. LinkU is meant to be an easy way to meet new people and enjoy new experiences.
As part of the project I was in charge of the design at LinkU. Minor tasks included business development and market research in the early phases of the project. 

The logo design, the UI design and the UX of the app were part of my tasks, as well as the development of [Linku website][linku]. I produced and tested several designs mainly using Sketch. Different iterations of the UI designs were tested using Invision.
