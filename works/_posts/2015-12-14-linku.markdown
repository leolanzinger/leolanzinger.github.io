LinkU is a mobile application developed in 2015 in Paris for the Business Development Lab course at Université Paris-Sud, where I studied the first year of my Master degree. 
LinkU is about getting to know people and making new experience: it is indeed a location-based event platform.
<br />
I was head of the design at LinkU, but minor tasks also included business development and market research in the early phases of the project. 

![LinkU screens 1](/images/linku/linku_screens_1.png)
![LinkU screens 2](/images/linku/linku_screens_2.png)
*Some screenshots from LinkU*

The interface design went through different iterations, starting from an <span style="color: #00BCD0;">**azure**</span> design and ending up to a much more intense and emotional <span style="color: #F44336;">**red**</span>. 
<br />
The whole design was meant to give to the user the most efficient and easy interface to meet new people: rather than searching for the right perfect event, the user interface would prompt the events of the preferred categories that are happening around one user location. 
<br />
After all, the motto of the app was **'because moments are around you'**.

![LinkU logo](/images/linku/logo-white.png)
*The final LinkU logo design*

I also worked on the logo design and the development of [getlinku.com][linku]. The final logo represents a cute hedgehog that is looking outside his den, just like what happens when we move to a new city and we look down the street to meet new friends.

I produced and tested several designs on Sketch and Invision. The design follows the Material Design guidelines.
